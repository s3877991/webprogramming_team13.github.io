    • About Us


    MAC is the largest makeup brand with more than 1000 stores in 89 countries. We are proud to be the favourite choice of many famous artitist and people all over the world.

    Our one and only guideline is All Ages, All Races, All genders. Therefore, you can find numerous makeup products with a wide range of shades from different categories. We are confident that we are the most diverse makeup authority

    We have the most enthusiastic and united community of the world's best makeup artists working together to bring the most extraordinary products to our customers.

    Established in March 1984, Toronto, Canada by Frank Toskan and Frank Angelo, MAC has gone through so many ups and downs to stand on the top of the world like today. Everything just started by making cosmetics in the kitchen, then their reputation grew constantly among the makeup artist community.
